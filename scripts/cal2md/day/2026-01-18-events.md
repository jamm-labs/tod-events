## Woodshop induction - January - part 2
- **Start:** 2026-01-18 10:00
- **End:** 2026-01-18 14:00
- **Location:** The Workshop, Tod College, Burnley Road, Todmorden, West Yorkshire, OL14 7BX, United Kingdom

The second of a two part course to learn to use the tools and equipment in the College’s new Woodshop. 

Learn how to use a Router Table, Planer/Thicknesser, Sander and Drill Press in the Woodshop at Tod College. Attendees will learn how to operate the machines in a safe manner. Maximum number of 4 attendees so everyone gets hands on experience. This course is particularly suitable for confident beginners and hobbyist woodworkers. The course is mandatory for those who wish to use these machines in the Woodshop at future open sessions.
