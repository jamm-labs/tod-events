Play Development Services provide free open access sessions for children and young people, a place to try out new activities and making new friends whilst having fun! 

We provide inclusive a sport and play. We have a range of activities that are chosen by the children and young people. 

The activities sometimes get messy, so please come in clothes that are appropriate for the activities and the weather. We aim for all our activities to be inclusive to all. 

We operate on a first come, first served basis as places. In our busy periods we advise you to come early to avoid disappointment as places are limited are are filled quickly. 

We ask children and young people with SEND to book so that we can meet their particular needs.
