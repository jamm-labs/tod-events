Join us every Wednesday for a relaxed and creative life drawing session! Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced artist, this group is open to everyone who wants to practice their drawing skills in a relaxed and supportive environment. Grab your sketchbook and pencils and come unleash your creativity with us! 

Sessions are untutored and self directed. 

Boards and Easels are provided, materials are not, but you are welcome to bring anything you would like. There are sinks in the room for paint/ink/clay etc. 

Please contact Sophie about any access requirements and she will do her best to accommodate you.
