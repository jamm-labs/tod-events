A support group for those with a diagnosis of prostate cancer, whether recently diagnosed and wondering what happens next or those further through their cancer journey. 

The group is LGBTQ+ inclusive and anyone with a diagnosis of prostate cancer is welcome to attend. 

For more information or to register your interest, please contact Dave. You can also visit the website.
