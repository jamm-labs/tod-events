Kixx is a children’s football academy, who provide a high-energy, fun, and imaginative introduction to physical activity, all through the power of football. 

Kixx sessions are also a brilliant way for children to build friendships and grow in confidence. Every class is carefully designed for both boys and girls aged 18 months to 10 years, ensuring the activities are age-appropriate, inclusive, and engaging. 

The Kixx curriculum focuses on developing both physical skills and social skills — helping every child learn, move, and thrive in a supportive environment. 

Our vision is simple: to inspire every child to be better through football.
