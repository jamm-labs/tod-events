Join Debbie Bartholomew for Iyengar Yoga classes in Todmorden! Whatever your experience or ability come along to class and learn the art of Iyengar Yoga in a fun and relaxed environment. 

Iyengar Yoga is a long established approach which helps students to build confidence, fitness and wellbeing.  In class we focus on alignment, precision, extension and breath which helps improve the sense of ease in mind and body. 

Debbie is an extremely knowledgable teacher with over 30 years experience.  She holds the level 3 Iyengar teaching qualification and the worldwide Iyengar Yoga certification mark which assures quality and excellence in teaching.
