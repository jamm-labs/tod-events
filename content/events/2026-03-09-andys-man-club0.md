Andys Man Club offer a free to attend peer-to-peer support group every Monday, except bank holidays. In these groups, men can open up about the storms affecting their lives in a safe, judgement-free and non-clinical environment. These clubs are designed to be free of pressure, there is no obligation for men to speak, they can simply listen if they wish. We know it can be daunting contemplating coming to a club with people you don’t know, but don’t be afraid to come along and see for yourself. It might be the best decision you make. Please remember this group is 18+ 

ANDYSMANCLUB are a men’s suicide prevention charity that aims to end the stigma surrounding men’s mental health and help men through the power of conversation. #ITSOKAYTOTALK
